Summarizer works on the principle that the least important sentences will consist almost entirely if not entirely of filler words, words that are short and common. These sentences can be discarded without losing the meaning of the text. Therefore, Summarizer splits the text into sentences, scores each word by calculating its length and the number of times it occurs in the text, and adds up the scores of each word in each sentence to get a total score for each sentence. Summarizer then discards the sentences that have a less than average score, and outputs the result.
